- kan vara means reduca poverty, öka social justice och individual well-being

Digital aspect är däremot absent från review on decent work

### Theoretical perspectives on decent work
Finns tre perspektiv det tolkas ur: economic perspective/macro level, self-value/psychological perspective, management and organizational perspectives
#### economic perspective/macro level
Saker som kan användas för att mäta decent work:

- employment, 
- social security, 
- social dialog,
- human rights, 
(väldigt många snarlika)

Kritik mot universality of the concept of good work:
- Olika länder har olika faktorer som påverkar det, ändrar i hur man mäter decent work
- statistiska indicators har ibland inte sufficient practical implications
- För vissa personer betyder definitionen av good work olika saker

#### Self-value/psychological perspective
Bluestien introduced decent work from vocational psychology-perspective
- Menar work vital part of human life, och att organizations ska strive to provide decent work for all
- Psychology of Working Theory (PWT)
	- Emphasizes interplay of contextual factors with psychological factors that contributes to individual experience of decent work
	- *KEy copmonents of decent work:*
		• Physical and interpersonal safety: this encompasses the freedom from physical, mental, and emo-
		tional abuse at work.
		• adequate rest and leisure time: Maintaining a healthy work-life balance is crucial.
		• Organizational values that support family and social values: workplaces should promote policies that
		respect employees’ personal lives.
		• adequate compensation: Fair wages are essential for meeting basic needs and financial security.
		• Provision of appropriate health services: access to health care is a key factor in overall well-being.
- Researchers har märkt att olika faktorer spelar olika mycket roll för uppfattningen av decent work över olika länder

Decent work questionnaire covers seven key areas:
- the DwQ covers seven key areas: fair treatment, reasonable working hours, meaningful work, fair pay, social protection, growth opportunities, and health and safety. 

#### Management and organizational perspectives
Decent work as a management conceptär result av employes evaluation of their daily work and work envirnment, hard/soft factors in work, all aspects of work, and the quality of employment and equality in the work environment.
- research fokuserar på hur managers kan fostera/implementa decent work practices within organizations
- Policies that prioritize well being, growth, positive work environments
- dåligt harmar organizations
- Well-designed job ökar cognitive capacity och performance, meets human needs väl

## Thematic analysis
**Digital work transformation**
- digital solutions ökar sense of control och autonomy
- tillåter mindre tasks, projects, temporary employment. Routine tasks minskar vilket ger higher order cognitive activities,. Å andra sidan minskar job security med temporary employment
- Mer gig economy, företag använder mer contractors, mindre job security, vilket kan negatively affect well being

**Inequitable compensation**
- fair compensation ökar healthcare etc, bra
- är också linked to increased employee motivation, job satisfaction etc
- Gig economy dåligt för fair compensation
	- Low, unpredictable wages

**Work fulfilment**
- är när både personal och proffesional needs möts
- decent work har positiv impact på well being
- poor working conditions ökar risken för accidents, anxeity, depression, etc
- Bra conditions bra för well being eftersom man får self determination needs, social contribution needs, connection etc
	- får bra relationsships och autonomy, motivated to learn new things
