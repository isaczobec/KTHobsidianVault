Shore et al define inclusion som "the degree to which an employee perceives that he or she is an esteemed member of the work group through uniqueness"

- Byggd på optimal distinctivesness theory: folk vill vara både lika och olika från andra samtidigt
- Similarity ökar sannolikhet bli welcomed i group och belonging, difference related to recognition of ways in which person is distinctive, ökar need for fullfilment.
- Ökar fullfilment att ha starka relationships med work group, acceptans av grupp de identifyar med
- Belonging till group kräver dock compliance till vissa normer som kan strida mot need for uniqueness - om unika drag hos människor upplevs som devalued minskar kännsla av inclusion.

Shore et al presenterade 2x2 inclusion framework för beskriva hur belongingness och value in uniqueness upplevs i grupp.

- Inclusion = high belongingness och value in uniqueness - kan ha individuality medan treated som insider
- Assimilation = high belongingness and low value in uniqueness - när employee upplevs som insider endast då de passar in i organizational behavioural norms by minimizng display of ways in which they are unique
- Differentiation = low belongingness and high value in uniqueness när employee inte viewed som insider men dess unika characteristics ses som viktiga för gruppens success (kan uppleva om ex är diversity hire)
- Exclusion = low belongingness and low value in uniqueness; när en employee inte ses som viktig insider med viktig roll, men andra employees är insiders.

- Leader kommunicerar dominant culture genom behaviour, feedback, mindset, coaching etc, och newcomers kommer lära sig hur de ska acta från detta för conforma.

### The leader emphasizing exclusion
Self-categorization theory = people describe self concept in terms of their membership in various groups
- members skapar identity, conform their connection to group by showing partiality to members of their own category
- kan minska interaction mellan diverse groups
- categorization kan öka work group animosity och in- out-group membership
- When targets socially excluded by leader kan känna inte worthy av acknowledgement eller respectful treatment
	- om inte conformar till group norms kan man bli excluded
	- leader måste se till att endast bra normer spelar roll (dock normer sällan medvetna, evolvar själva och kan skada folk)
- Typer av social exclusion:
	- Rejection
		- negative attention eller indicate someone är unwanted
		- leader reffering to someone in demeaning way, laugh at someone, etc
	- Microaggressions
		- brief/subtle comments / behaviours that members of minority group often experience on daily basis
		- inte alltid intended
		- tre typer
			- microassult
				- explicit racial derogations
				- intented to hurt recipient
			- microinsult
				- unconcious unintended
				- comment/act that conveys rudeness, insensitivity or degrading
			- microinvalidation
				- unconcious unintended
				- actions that nullify views, experiences 
		- ger Mental och physical harm t recipient
	- Ostracism
		- När individual or group omit to take actions that engage another organizational member when it is socially appropriate to do so
		- ignored, unanswered greetings, etc
		- ostracism negatively related to core performance, helping, voice, positively related till turnover (intentions)
		- three leader purposes for ostracism:
			- protect group from problematic members
			- reveal to individuals that behaviour needs modification to remain in group
			- remove deviant individuals who resist correction
- microagressions ofta specifically associated with marginalized groups
- leader exclusion can have harmful effects
	- ibland realizar inte att gör microagressions
	- förhindrar bygga relationships, får inte de positiva benefitsen av detta; ingen belonging
	- leader kanske assumar att recipient kommer lära sig en läxa och managa sitt behaviour för att complya (med norms), men approach kan väl ha fler negativa effects
	- exclusive leader kan också alienata andra employees som obserar exclusions
		- more likely att se leader som malicious om employeen är dissimilair, devalue the leader
		- reduce leader trust
		- microaggresions vanligast i white-dominant organization
		- exclusion by white male leader mot marginalized group ses ofta som discrimination, kan öka turnover
			- kan leda till lawsuits, hurta organizations repuation, plaintiffs etc
